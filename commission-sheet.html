Process

  1. You contact me (preferable on Instagram).
  2. You tell me all the information under in "Information I need from you".
  3. I will check if I have the skins, objects or scenery required, and if I can do it (difficulty).
  4. We calculate how much it is, and you pay me in desired method.
  5. I will start working on your project and I will show you how is going to see if you like it.
  6. I render the final image/s (I like to do different angles).
  7. I will need an email to send you the final image/s. I will provide the images with no watermark and with watermark.
  8. If you want to post it, make sure to credit me with a mention and tagging.
  9. In case I like your commission and I want to post it, I will ask you first if you are okay with it.

Pictures of reference: Pose and face expression
